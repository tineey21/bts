Food Nutrients

"Healthy eating is a way of life, so it's important to establish routines that are simple, realistically, and ultimately livable."

Beef

Beef provides essential nutrients like protein, iron, and B vitamins, supporting muscle growth and overall health. Moderation and choosing lean cuts are advisable.

Bread

Bread is a convenient source of carbohydrates and fiber, offering quick energy and aiding digestion. Opt for whole grains for added nutritional benefits.

Fish

Fish is a high-quality protein source rich in omega-3 fatty acids, supporting heart health and providing essential nutrients. Incorporating fish in your diet promotes overall well-being.

Chicken

Chicken is a lean protein source, rich in essential nutrients like protein, vitamins, and minerals. It supports muscle development and overall health when part of a balanced diet.

Pork

Pork is a good source of protein, B vitamins, and minerals. Including it in moderation can contribute to muscle health and overall nutrient intake.

Pasta

Pasta provides quick energy through carbohydrates and is a versatile base for nutritious meals. Opt for whole-grain options for added fiber and nutrients.
